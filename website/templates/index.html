Clare Archibald

Clare Archibald is a Scottish multidisciplinary writer-artist working with text, sound, image, materials, and objects. Her work explores place, time, articulation, movement and encounter. She is interested in the intersections and between spaces of the autodidactic and the academic, the private and the public, and the individual and the collective.

This website is currently being rebuilt and restructured to streamline her large body of diverse work, residency and event history, commissions and awards.
